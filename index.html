From Walls to Skin

Street Art Heritage

With over 8 years of tattooing experience across Poland, Germany, and Switzerland, I bring a unique perspective that merges street art sensibilities with fine tattoo artistry.

Starting from graffiti at a young age, I've been creating large-scale murals for 15 years, which naturally evolved into the precision and artistry of tattooing.

This background in street art and murals gives me a unique perspective on composition, flow, and how art interacts with both urban environments and the human form.

My work spans from intimate 10cm tattoos to building-sized murals, always maintaining the same attention to detail and artistic vision whether working on skin or concrete.
